Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 June 2025 · Last Updated: 10 September 2025

This Privacy Policy covers two Discord bots operated by Sic4rioDragon (the “Operator”):

  • TAW DayZ Division Bot (“Activity Bot”)
  • TAW Division Ticket Bot (“Ticket Bot”)

The bots are operator-managed. Only the Operator (and any authorized bot team members) deploys/configures them.

1) Who Controls Your Data

  • For processing performed directly by the bots and for storage on the Operator’s infrastructure, the Operator is the data controller.
  • If a server admin requests exports (e.g., Google Sheets) or maintains their own copies/archives, that admin may act as an independent controller for that exported data.

2) What We Collect

Common (both bots)

  • Discord identifiers: User ID, username, display name; Guild/Server ID, Channel ID, message/interaction IDs.
  • Timestamps of interactions and technical diagnostics (uptime, rate limits, errors).

Activity Bot

  • Voice state events (join/leave, channel IDs) and derived durations (lifetime / weekly / monthly minutes).
  • Optional presence/game info only if explicitly enabled in configuration.

Ticket Bot

  • Ticket content (messages in ticket channels or threads), ticket participants, timestamps, and status changes.
  • Attachments uploaded within tickets (filename, size, MIME type, CDN URL or storage path).
  • Transcripts/archives of tickets and moderation notes where enabled by admins.

We do not intentionally collect emails or real-world identifiers. Identification is via Discord accounts/IDs.

3) Why We Process Data (Legal Bases)

  • Legitimate interests: operate support/moderation workflows; provide community features; prevent abuse; secure the service.
  • Consent: opening/participating in a ticket; using optional/opt-in features.
  • Contract / Requested service: responding to support requests and providing bot functions you ask for.
  • Legal obligations: responding to valid law-enforcement requests when required by law.

4) How We Use Data

  • Provide, maintain, and improve bot functionality.
  • Calculate and display activity statistics (aggregated voice minutes, etc.).
  • Operate ticket workflows, including transcripts/archives for authorized staff.
  • Operator access for audit/troubleshooting (see ToS §11): limited to what’s necessary; least-privilege; not used for unrelated monitoring.
  • Debugging, reliability, and safety (rate-limit handling, abuse/threat mitigation).
  • Optional exports/sync (e.g., Google Sheets) or self-hosted storage if configured.

We do not sell your data. We share data only as described below.

5) Storage, Sharing, and Transfers

  • Storage: local files/databases on the Operator’s server/infrastructure. Ticket attachments may reside on Discord CDN or self-hosted storage when configured.
  • Sharing: with Discord (APIs/CDN); authorized server staff for moderation/support; Google only if a server/admin enables Sheets syncing; and hosting providers used by the Operator.
  • No advertising sharing: we do not share with advertisers or data brokers.
  • Transfers: providers may process data outside your country. We rely on the providers’ contractual safeguards where applicable.

6) Retention

Defaults (may be adjusted per-server/config):

  • Activity aggregates: retained while the feature is active or until purged by admin request.
  • Ticket transcripts: up to 24 months.
  • Ticket attachments: up to 180 days, or subject to Discord CDN availability.
  • Technical logs: up to 90 days, unless needed longer for security/debugging.

Minimal records may be kept to honor opt-out or deletion requests.

7) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and port your data.

  • Requests require verification via your Discord account (we may ask for your User ID and the server name/ID).
  • If data is stored/exported by a server admin, we may direct you to that admin for those copies.

8) Security

We apply reasonable measures for a small, self-hosted project: token protection, least-privilege permissions, limited access, and logging controls. No system is 100% secure.

9) Children

The bots are not intended for users under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe we unintentionally processed such data, contact us to delete it.
