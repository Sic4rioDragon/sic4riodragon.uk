Who we are

Let us introduce ourselves, we are Ride ’til we Rot Society (RTWR/Rotters). We are a non-profit charity that exists to support bikers & trikers who suffer with a varying degree of disabilities, irrespective of cause, be it illness, genetic or accident.

From humble beginnings RTWR has grown into an international support group. Initially started in Europe, we now exist in the USA, Australia, New Zealand and as far south as South Africa.

Our mission is to support & assist members in their charitable needs. We organise social meets, fund-raising events and always offer a bit of tongue-in-cheek humour.

The Society is non-affiliated; we aim to support all Clubs & organisations without competing with them. We have in the past offered support to MC’s & MCC’s. We are not a Motorcycle club.

Our members come from all walks & wheels of life from able-bodied, disabled all the way to severely disabled.
